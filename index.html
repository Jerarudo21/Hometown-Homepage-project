Top three activities to do at Ikeja

Get your groove on at Fela Shrine

Afrobeat music is taking over the world and the new afrika shrine known as the Fela Shrine is capital of the genre. Run by Femi & Yeni Kuti children of the legendary Fela Anikulapo Kuti.

See a movie, play video games and do more at ICM

Ikeja City Mall called ICM for short is a total entertainment centre. Retail center houses like the popular Silverbird cinemas, playzone, a large food court and other luxury retail brands are present

Eat out at Ikeja GRA

Ikeja GRA is an affluent neighbourhood in Ikeja. The area houses several restaurants and eateries The Yellow Chilli, Zen Garden, Orchid Bistro and more.
